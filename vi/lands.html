# Lands

In Wanaka Farm, there are different types of terrain and environment such as Basic, Forest, Sea, lake. We have released 15000 lands of the first terrain type - Basic Land. To get started, you should own at least one land. In addition, you can also participate in the game and make profits with the Default Land’s function, but it is more limited than owning a land.

# 1. Buy Lands

Wanna play this game to its fullest potential? You should buy at least one land. Lands sold by Wanaka Farm have a starting price for the first 15000 basic lands of $180 for each one, in WANA token at the time of purchase. (50 WANA at that time). Now, you can buy lands on Wanaka's secondary marketplace (https://marketplace.wanakafarm.com/ (opens new window)) and Babylons' market (https://app.babylons.io/wanaka (opens new window)).

# 2. Unpack

After buying a land box, you will unpack to find out the rarity of your land, and how many seasons there are on it.

# 3. Rarity

There are 6 rarities of land: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Legendary, Mythical and Immortal. Each land contains the percentage of two attributes: Reducing time of growth and percentage of mutation. Based on the rarity of land you own, harvesting time will be shortened. The rarity also helps to buff the mutation rate on your land. (Common land still has the default mutation rate from the start 0,25%)

# 4. Seasons

Imagine, with seasonal attributes, lands in Wanaka can only raise and grow particular products. For example, products in the tropics will not be able to grow in a cold environment. Here are the chance rate of season one land could has:

  • 1 season: 8%
  • 2 seasons: 60%
  • 3 seasons: 24%
  • 4 seasons: 8%

What makes a land unique is the lake on that land. There will be a land with 2 lakes, a land with a large lake and a land with only a small lake.

# 5. Land Level

Upgrading land does not change the rarity nor the season(s) attributed on your land. Leveling up for land only increases the number of farming slots and unlocks new items for farming. A land has a total of 289 farming plots. You will be started at level 1 with a total of 10 plots. Performing actions and spending time in the game will increase your exp points. If you reach specific experience levels, you may have to burn WAI coins to upgrade your land's level. (some items or WANA coins may also be required at certain specific levels). At this stage, the maximum level of a land is 50 for 289 farming plots. Later, we will update specific details of how many $WAI should be burnt for each level.

Maximum Land Level will be unlocked by stages, before each level: 10, 20, 30, 40, 50. In the first stage, you can only upgrade the land to level 9. After reaching level 9, they need to wait for level 10's unlock event. However, your experience will be accumulated. Experience can only be obtained by harvesting growing items on the land. Gained experience depends on the times of watering and feeding.

# 6. Default Lands

There will be no Free-to-Play. You’ll still have to invest $WANA in order to open 4 plots on Default Land (Field, Planting, Pond, Farming). Each land plot costs 20USD in $WANA token (at real-time exchange rate) to open. After that, like many other farmers, you still have to invest to buy seeds and stuff which are available in WanaShop. Default Land also has the ability to produce mutated items which is 0,25%. There are also a few daily quests taking place on default land for all players. Default land can not be upgraded, but we have full seasons on default land.
