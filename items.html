# Items

# 1. Seed items

Whether you own land or play on the Default land, you need to buy seed packs to start your farming. Seed packs packs will be sold on WanaShop in $WANA (this price is fixed by USD value at the time of your purchase). In the release version, there are 4 seed items: Cabbage, Apple, Cow and Koi Fish. More items will be added to the game soon.

Each seed pack contains 2 items. An account can buy a maximum of 3 seed packs from WanaShop. Then, you have to reproduce the breed yourself by Breed or buy from other players on WanaTrade market.

# 2. Growing items

After you have bought a seed and then start to raise, those items that are in the process of growth are called Growing items. At this stage you should water plants and feed animals. It’s okay if you leave them without watering or feeding. But remember, their quality and their weight determine your harvested products' value. Also, no one knows at the start which item will be mutated, but there’s one thing you should know: high quality mutated items bring more WAI.

# 3. Harvested items

These are harvested products, which can be sold or reused to take Breed or Produce new products. Harvested items have different product quality metrics (stars, weight...) which determine the value of each product. In addition, you can receive mutated products that bring you WAI coins. Some daily quests will ask you to collect enough harvested items to receive the reward. Like Seed items, harvested items can be traded on WanaTrade.

# 4. Product items

Harvested products which have been brought into the processing factory to create new products, are called Product items. Not only help you to accomplish rewarded quests, but these items can also increase or decrease some of your activities in the game.

# 5. Mutated items

Once you start farming, it might give you mutated items, which are NFT, once mutated that item, will not be able to breed or produce anymore. However, you can replant it on the land as a form of staking. Each mutated item has 2 factors of quality as well as weight, these 2 factors depend on whether you take care of watering / feeding frequently or not.Quality and weight will determine the total amount of a certain $WAI that it will generate on your land. However, this $WAI will be daily generated and will stop when fully paid to you. In addition, when you own certain special mutated items, you will have the right to participate in our events and mini games. When mutated items no longer produce $WAI, when there is a special trade-in Event you can use the required number of your mutated items in exchange for a new mutated item. (e.g you could use 3 of your mutated items in exchange for a new one). Each growing item has his own mutation rate. The base rate is 0,25%. Each time 50 items grow without mutation, the rate will increase x2. In case a mutated item appears, the mutation rate will be reset.

# 6. Building items

We have items to use for farm decoration. As these items are usually FT with unlimited quantity, you can use WAI to buy those on WanaShop. There are also special limited-edition building items that will be NFTs sold in WANA, and these collectible and decorative items will be sold or rewarded at special events.

# 7. Ingredient items

There are small items such as salt, pepper, sugar,.. that will be sold on the shop using WAI so that you can use it to create product items.

# 8. Collectible items

# Characters

When the game starts, you will be given 1 default character. In addition, you can go to the market to buy other characters for 9 USD in $WANA token. There will be characters that are sold in limited quantities later, we will work with partners to create new collectible characters sold at a higher price.

# Pets

Similar to the characters, Pets will be sold on the market. Currently Pets only have the properties of being friends with the character, following your character for the purpose of collecting and decorating. Later, there may be changes and additions to the properties for Pets. In the future, there will be pets that are sold in limited quantities, we will cooperate with partners to create new Pets that are collectible and sold at a higher price or used for gift or reward. For example: Brawlio88, Kmanus88's cat and Duckdao's duck that testers met in the Beta Test. There are 100 Duckdao ducks for 100 lucky people in the last Duckdao birthday event. Brawlio88 will be open for sale in the near future with limited quantities.
