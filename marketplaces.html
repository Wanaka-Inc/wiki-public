# Marketplaces

# WanaShop

At WanaShop you can buy products from game manufacturers in both $WANA and $WAI. Here are the products you can find at WanaShop:

  • Seed pack
  • Building items
  • Ingredient items
  • Character
  • Pet

# WanaTrade

This is a place to exchange and trade among players, as players can not only trade and sell their assets, but also create offers to buy assets from others and give them to their friends. All transactions on WanaTrade will be traded in $WANA.
