# ACTIVITIES

# Breeding

To have seed for farming, you can buy it on the market, buy it from another player, or recreate it yourself by using 2 harvested items to breed. Breeding will cost WAI. And there will be a random chance of being given 2-4 seeds per one breeding.

Breeding rate depends on the amount of watering and feeding.

  • Cabbage: 6 WAI and 6 Energy to breed
  • Apple: 12 WAI and 12 Energy to breed
  • Koin Fish: 18 WAI and 18 Energy to breed
  • Cow: 24 WAI and 24 Energy to breed

# Producing

After harvesting, you can use your harvested items to create product items to do daily quests or to boost your productivity. This process will require some ingredients sold on the WanaShop, you have to burn 5 WAI and 2 Energy.

# Energy

Each player will have 480 energy from the start, and energy regenerates over time for 30 energy per hour. In addition, you can use product items to restore energy. Energy will be used to perform operations in the game.
