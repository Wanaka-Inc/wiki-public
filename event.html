# EVENT

In addition to the main gameplay, we are always associated with special events in real life such as Halloween, Xmas, New Year, Valentine... The most recent event that will take place is Halloween. Our farm will be decorated with a new theme during the event. There will be separate items for each event that you can gain in the event's minigame, or by purchase.
